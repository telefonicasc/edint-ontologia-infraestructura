Abstract

The purpose of this ontology is to provide a common vocabulary for representing the main entities and data of a municipality's infrastructure. This data includes educational centers, cultural and social centers, sports centers, and health centers, as well as on- and off-street parking. Its scope is limited to data that can be used to maintain and access the inventory of municipal facilities and to track their resource consumption (electricity, water, etc.) and to manage mobility (pedestrian access to municipal facilities and vehicle access to parking areas), which are part of the routine functions of local authorities.
